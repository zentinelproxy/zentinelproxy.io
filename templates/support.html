Support Sentinel

Sentinel stays open source. You pay for assurance and operational deliverables.

Sentinel exists to keep critical web infrastructure boring, inspectable, and shared. We believe security-focused edge infrastructure should be transparent, forkable, and free from vendor lock-in.

Your support funds CI/CD infrastructure, security audits, documentation, and the maintainers who keep Sentinel production-ready. Whether you're a solo developer or an enterprise team, there's a way to contribute.
