Support Zentinel

Zentinel stays open source. You pay for assurance and operational deliverables.

Zentinel exists to keep critical web infrastructure boring, inspectable, and shared. We believe security-focused edge infrastructure should be transparent, forkable, and free from vendor lock-in.

Your support funds CI/CD infrastructure, security audits, documentation, and the maintainers who keep Zentinel production-ready. Whether you're a solo developer or an enterprise team, there's a way to contribute.

Enterprise Builds

Production-ready artifacts for teams that need supply chain security, compliance documentation, and long-term stability. All releases already include signed binaries, SBOMs, and SLSA provenance for free. Enterprise adds LTS, early advisories, and config stability on top.

Signed Artifacts

Binaries signed with Sigstore/cosign with full provenance attestation. Verify authenticity before deployment.

SBOM & Vulnerability Reports

SPDX and CycloneDX software bill of materials. Automated vulnerability scanning with each release.

LTS Releases

Long-term support branches tied to CalVer series (e.g., 26.01 LTS). 12-month security backport windows. Patch releases stay on the same series — no feature churn.

Config Stability

Guaranteed configuration compatibility within an LTS window. A config that works on 26.01_0 works on 26.01_12.

Security Advisories

Early notification of security issues with patch timelines. Private disclosure before public announcement.

Update Channel

Automated update notifications with checksums. Integrate with your deployment pipeline.
